        Here's a bit more detail on the currencies of some major countries:

            United States: 
            Currency - United States Dollar (USD). 
            Symbol: $. 
            Code: USD. 
            The USD is the world's primary reserve currency, widely used in international trade and finance.
            
            United Kingdom: 
            Currency - Pound Sterling (GBP). 
            Symbol: £. 
            Code: GBP.
            The GBP is one of the oldest currencies still in use today and is commonly referred to as the "pound."
            
            European Union: 
            Currency - Euro (EUR). 
            Symbol: €. Code: EUR. 
            The EUR is used by 19 of the 27 European Union member states, making it one of the most widely used currencies in the world.
            
            Japan: 
            Currency - Japanese Yen (JPY). 
            Symbol: ¥. 
            Code: JPY. 
            The JPY is known for its stability and is the third most traded currency in the foreign exchange market.
            
            China: 
            Currency - Chinese Yuan Renminbi (CNY). 
            Symbol: ¥. 
            Code: CNY. 
            The CNY is controlled by the People's Bank of China and is becoming increasingly important in global trade and finance.
            
            India: 
            Currency - Indian Rupee (INR). 
            Symbol: ₹. 
            Code: INR. 
            The INR is issued and regulated by the Reserve Bank of India and is widely used in South Asia.
            
            Canada: 
            Currency - Canadian Dollar (CAD). 
            Symbol: $. 
            Code: CAD. 
            The CAD is known for its correlation with the price of oil due to Canada's significant oil reserves and exports.
            
            Australia: 
            Currency - Australian Dollar (AUD). 
            Symbol: $. 
            Code: AUD. 
            The AUD is often considered a commodity currency due to Australia's abundance of natural resources.
            
            Switzerland: 
            Currency - Swiss Franc (CHF). 
            Symbol: CHF. 
            Code: CHF. 
            The CHF is known for its stability and is often used as a safe-haven currency during times of global economic uncertainty.
            
            Russia: 
            Currency - Russian Ruble (RUB). 
            Symbol: ₽. 
            Code: RUB. 
            The RUB is issued by the Central Bank of Russia and is influenced by fluctuations in oil prices and geopolitical events.
